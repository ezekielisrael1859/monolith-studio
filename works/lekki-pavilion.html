04 Commercial Lekki, Lagos

Lekki Pavilion

An open-air events pavilion designed for a private estate, built to host everything from small gatherings to large receptions without ever needing internal partitions. The structure itself does the work — a cantilevered steel roof provides shade and rain cover while keeping the space visually open on all sides.

The brief prioritised flexibility over permanence, resulting in a pavilion that can be reconfigured for a hundred different events without altering its architecture.

Location Lekki, Lagos
Type Commercial
Area 460 m²
Year 2025
Cantilevered steel roof, main event space Open glass edge, garden-facing side Polished concrete flooring, event floor

"A space that disappears when it needs to, and holds its own when it doesn't."

Estate Management, Client
