02 Residential Banana Island, Lagos

Banana Island House

Commissioned for a family that wanted quiet over spectacle, this house rejects the ornamental language common to the island in favour of long horizontal planes and deep roof overhangs that shade the interior year-round.

Internally, spaces flow into one another with minimal partitioning — a deliberate response to a brief that asked for a home that felt "unfinished, but complete."

Location Banana Island, Lagos
Type Residential
Area 890 m²
Year 2024
Timber screening along the western elevation Open stair connecting living and family floors Glass facade facing the internal courtyard
