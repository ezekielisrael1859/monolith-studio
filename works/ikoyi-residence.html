Ikoyi Residence

A private family home built around a single idea: light should be earned, not given freely. The residence unfolds around a central concrete courtyard, with living spaces arranged to catch morning sun and retreat into shadow by afternoon.

Material honesty guided every decision — poured concrete, timber, and glass left largely untreated, allowing the building to age visibly and gracefully over time.

Location Ikoyi, Lagos
Type Residential
Area 640 m²
Year 2025
Concrete stair connecting private and communal floors Living space, courtyard-facing elevation Afternoon light study, upper floor
