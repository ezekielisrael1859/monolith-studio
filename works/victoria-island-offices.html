03 Commercial Victoria Island, Lagos

Victoria Island Offices

A four-floor office fit-out for a financial services firm that wanted to move away from the glass-and-chrome language typical of the district. The brief called for a workplace that felt considered rather than corporate.

Exposed concrete ceilings, muted material palettes, and generous circulation space were used to slow the pace of the interior — proof that commercial architecture doesn't have to shout to command a room.

Location Victoria Island, Lagos
Type Commercial
Area 2,100 m²
Year 2024
Exposed steel structure, reception floor Concrete ceiling finish, open-plan floor Shared workspace, third floor

"Our office finally feels like the brand we've built. Quiet, but unmistakable."

C. Nwosu, Client
